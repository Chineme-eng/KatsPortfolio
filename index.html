About Me

Hey — I'm KP. I build digital things: web tools that save time, templates that look great out of the box, guides that actually teach, and startups that scratch real itches.

My work lives at the intersection of design and utility. Not interested in bloated software or filler — just sharp, focused products that do exactly what they say.

Everything here started with a problem I personally had. From Picao AI (an AI workspace for creators) to Freelance Calculators, Tax Maps, and Coding Guides — all built from scratch, all solving real things.

Find my products on Gumroad, connect on LinkedIn, and explore the code on GitHub.
